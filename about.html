About Me

Here's what I'm up to.

Hello world!

I'm Isa, a curious soul venturing into the captivating realm of coding and web design. With a passion for learning and a blank canvas, I'm here on Skillshare to embark on my exciting journey.

Web design has always intrigued me, but I'm starting from scratch. As a complete beginner, I'm eager to unlock the secrets of crafting visually stunning and user-friendly websites. Each line of code I learn and every design principle I discover feels like a thrilling adventure.

While I may be new to this universe, I'm armed with determination and a commitment to growth. I'm excited to grasp the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, translating my ideas into digital reality. With every Skillshare lesson, I take one step closer to making my web design dreams a reality.

Join me on this exhilarating journey as we explore the magic of coding and web design together. Let's build beautiful websites from the ground up!
